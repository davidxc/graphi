FAQ

Is Graphi free to use?

Yes, Graphi is free to use. Please see the Terms of Service for the license and liability disclaimer.

What profilers does Graphi support?

Graphi currently only supports output from Python's cProfile. Support will be extended
to other profilers in the future, probably starting with profilers for Ruby and C.

What do you do with uploaded profiler output?

It's saved to disk so the application can generate the directed graph. Beyond that, I don't do
anything with the profiler output. I periodically delete uploaded files.
